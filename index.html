What makes us special?

Algoartians is an open-source, non-financial organization. We believe that knowledge should be freely accessible to all. We have unique and vibrant community that is dedicated to nurturing and empowering Bhutanese developers and aspiring coders. Our commitment is to create an inclusive and supportive community that transcends financial barriers..

At Algoartians, we believe that by coming together, we can create a brighter future for Bhutan's tech industry. Whether you're a beginner taking your first coding steps or an experienced developer looking to give back, Algoartians welcomes you with open arms.Anyone with a passion for coding is welcome to join us
